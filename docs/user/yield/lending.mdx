## How Lending on Llamalend Works

When you lend assets on Llamalend, you deposit them into a **lending market**. Each market lets users borrow one asset by using another as collateral, for example, borrowing crvUSD by using CRV as collateral. You earn interest as assets are borrowed. You can withdraw your assets anytime, as long as there's enough available (unborrowed) in the pool.

Interest rates on Llamalend are **dynamic**. They change with supply and demand. High demand for borrowing means higher interest for lenders; low demand means lower interest.

## Earning Yield as a Lender

As a lender on Llamalend, your primary sources of yield comes from:

* **Borrowing Interest:** This is the direct interest paid by borrowers for using your supplied assets. This interest automatically accrues to your deposited assets, increasing their value over time.
* **CRV Rewards:** Similar to providing liquidity in swap pools, some Llamalend markets may offer additional CRV tokens as rewards for supplying assets. These incentives encourage more capital to flow into the lending markets.
* **Other Token Rewards and Points:** Partner projects might also provide their own tokens or points as additional rewards for lenders in specific markets, further boosting your potential earnings.

Lending on Llamalend is an attractive option for users looking to earn yield on their crypto assets.
