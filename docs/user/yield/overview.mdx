Curve offers many different ways to earn and grow using your assets, including:

- [**Providing Liquidity to DEX Pools**](lp.mdx):
    By depositing your assets into a swap pool, you become a **liquidity provider (LP)**, and your assets are used to facilitate trades on the Curve decentralized exchange (DEX).  In return, you earn a fee each time a trade happens, and can stake your LP tokens for potentially more rewards, including CRV.

- [**Earning by Lending on Llamalend**](lending.mdx):
    Llamalend, allows you to **earn interest by lending your crypto assets** to borrowers. Simply deposit your assets into a lending market, and you'll start earning yield. The interest rates adjust dynamically based on how much supply is available and how much demand there is from borrowers.  Many markets also award CRV rewards and other incentives.

- **Earning with veCRV**:
    veCRV, or vote-escrowed CRV (locked CRV) is a key component for maximizing your earning potential and participating in the Curve ecosystem. By locking your CRV tokens for a chosen period, you receive veCRV, which unlocks two powerful benefits

    1. [**Claiming a Share of Curve's Protocol Revenue**](revenue.mdx):
        As a veCRV holder, you are entitled to a **share of the revenue generated by the Curve protocol**. A portion of the fees from DEX trades and crvUSD borrowing is collected and distributed weekly to veCRV holders. This allows you to directly benefit from the overall activity and trading volume across the Curve platform.

    2. [**Boost Your CRV Rewards (Up to 2.5x)**](boosting.mdx):
    If you're already earning CRV tokens from providing liquidity or lending, you can significantly **increase these rewards up to 2.5 times** The more veCRV you hold and the longer you lock your CRV, the higher your boost will be on your eligible CRV earnings. This means more CRV tokens for the same amount of liquidity provided or assets lent.

- [**crvUSD Savings Vault (scrvUSD)**](scrvusd.mdx):
    The scrvUSD vault offers a simple way to earn yield on your **crvUSD stablecoin**. When you deposit crvUSD, you receive **scrvUSD** tokens. These scrvUSD tokens automatically increase in value over time, as they accrue interest from the fees generated by crvUSD borrowing on Llamalend. It's like a high-yield savings account for crvUSD!
