# CRV Supply and Distribution

## Total Supply Allocation

The total supply of CRV is 3,030,030,299 tokens, distributed as follows:

| Allocation | Percentage | Amount (CRV) |
|------------|------------|--------------|
| Community Emissions | 62% | 1,878,618,785 |
| Team | 30% | 909,009,090 |
| Investors | 3% | 90,900,909 |
| Employees | 5% | 151,501,515 |

## Token Launch

The CRV token was launched on August 13, 2020, with an initial supply of 1,273,000,000 tokens. The distribution at launch was:

- 5% to early liquidity providers
- 3% to investors
- 5% to employees
- 30% to the team
- 57% to the community
