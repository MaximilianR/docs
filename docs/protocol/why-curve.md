title: Why Launch On Curve
---

Curve is one of the most battle-tested and widely integrated protocols in DeFi — a permissionless platform for building liquidity and lending markets without the need for asking for approval.

Whether you're launching a new stablecoin, scaling a already existing one, or designing yield strategies, Curve gives you access to the same infrastructure used by major protocols across the ecosystem. It's not just a venue for trading — it's a base layer for serious DeFi builders.

## Passive Liquidity & Permissionless Lending

Curve covers the full spectrum:

- **Algorithms for every pair** – specialised math for both stable (near-peg) and volatile assets, so almost any token can list and trade efficiently.  
- **Set-and-forget LPing** – deposit once and earn; no range-setting, rebalancing, or manual upkeep required.  
- **Permissionless markets** – anyone can spin up a liquidity pool or lending market with any collateral, no gatekeepers.
- **Comfort-first liquidations** – Curve’s LLAMMA mechanism unwinds positions gradually, enabling some of the highest LTVs in DeFi while giving borrowers time to react.  


## Liquidity Flywheel — Simple, Modular, Effective

Growing depth on Curve is deliberately easy for everyone:

- **Gauges** – whitelist your pool through a governance vote and direct some votes to it and CRV emissions will stream to it.
- **Bribe / Vote-Markets** – services such as Votium let you “rent” veCRV voting power instead of accumulating it yourself.
- **Permissionless LP Rewards** – drop your own incentive tokens straight to LPs via Curve’s reward contracts. No governance step required.

Mix any (or all) of these levers to spin up a self-reinforcing flow of liquidity. Everything is on-chain, transparent, and already battle-tested by leading projects, so you can focus on building rather than begging for listings.


## Deep Ecosystem Reach

Curve isn’t isolated — its pools plug directly into the rest of DeFi:

- **Aggregators** like 1inch, CowSwap, and Paraswap automatically route trades through Curve for best pricing.  
- **Yield platforms** like Convex and Yearn build on top of Curve pools, unlocking boosted rewards and capital efficiency.  
- **Multi-chain presence** – Curve runs on Ethereum mainnet plus leading L2s (Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, zkSync, and more), so liquidity is wherever users are.  
- **Wallet & dashboard integrations** – MetaMask, DeBank, Zapper, Arkham, and others surface Curve LP positions natively.

Once your pool is live, it becomes part of a composable system used by thousands of apps, strategies, and DAOs.


## Battle-Tested Since 2020

Curve has delivered four-plus years of uninterrupted service:

- **$4B+ peak TVL** across 15+ chains (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, zkSync, Polygon PoS, Avalanche, and more).  
- **>1,800 DAO votes** with no protocol-level downtime, securing upgrades and parameter tweaks transparently.  
- **Blue-chip reliance** — Frax (FRAX/USDC peg), Lido (stETH liquidity), MakerDAO (DAI stability), plus 50+ other projects build on Curve.  
- **Audits & live bug bounty** (TrailofBits, ChainSecurity, Quantstamp, Immunefi) reinforce security.
- **>$300 M in revenue** paid out to LPs and veCRV holders, demonstrating real, on-chain revenue.

A stable, transparent, and thoroughly audited base layer for teams who want to own their liquidity for the long haul.
